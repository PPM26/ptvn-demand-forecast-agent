**Goal:** Extract all meaningful **item names** mentioned in the user input.  
Items may refer to **physical products** or **services**.

### Extraction Rules
1. Identify and extract **specific product or service names** exactly as mentioned.
2. The input may contain **one or multiple items** — extract **all** of them.
3. **Do not include**:
   - quantities
   - model name
   - prices  
   - dates  
   - locations  
   - unrelated text  

   unless those details are **clearly part of the item name itself**.

4. Preserve the **original language and wording** (Thai, English, or mixed).
5. Examples of valid items:
   - `ค่าบริการpm`
   - `สบู่`

### Output Requirements
- Return **only the extracted item names**
- As a **list**
- Without additional commentary
